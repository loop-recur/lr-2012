fig. 1: we build apps

we don't provide unequaled solutions to optimize your this-or-that client-side whatever-you-call-it.

we build apps. good ones.
for web, mobile, and
tablet devices.

fig. 2: we don't take a top-oriented lateral approach, we build nice apps to pay the bills.

In plain english:

loop/recur is a small group of developers and designers working hard to build mobile and web apps that they can be proud of; for iOS, Android, and the web.

In more technical terms:

loop/recur's engineers build test-driven mobile back ends and front ends with great attention paid to user experience and design.

In useless buzzwords:

loop/recur collaboratively engineers data-driven mobile and cloud based applications to leverage the web 2.0 aspects of your business with a focus on scalability, best practices, and modularity. It's a win-win.

While our main technological choices are Javascript, Node.js, Ruby/Rails and Objective-C, our team has years of experience in multiple languages and platforms such as Haskell, Erlang, PHP, and Drupal.
