    Netgear NeoTV

    for iOS and Android

    The NeoTV Remote was built to control the Netgear NeoTV. Over wifi, the remote auto-connects via Bonjour and controls the onscreen cursor, keyboard, and navigation. The favorites feature allows you to save the channels you watch most from it's built in channel listing and allows you to post what you're watching to Facebook. Other features include vibration and sound feedback control, multiple box support and gesture control of onscreen functions.
